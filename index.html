Guidelines

We want your shout-out to look amazing, so please keep the following in mind:

  • While show program pages may not seem large at 5½″ × 8½″, photos do need to be high resolution in order to ensure they look good printed. Try to err on the side of too large, and we'll contact you if there are issues or questions about cropping.
  • Unless you've purchased a full-page shout-out, submitting collages or images containing more than one photo generally isn't a good idea.

  • It's generally a good idea to avoid including specific line breaks in your text:

    My Darling Angel: Congratulations on your first Highlands Theatre show! You're going to be amazing and we can't wait to see you shine on stage! - Love Mom & Dad

    works better than

    My Darling Angel:

    Congratulations on your first Highlands Theatre show!

    You're going to be amazing and we can't wait to see you shine on stage!

    - Love Mom & Dad
